I am a PhD candidate at UC San Diego, advised by Professors Xiaolong Wang and Hao Su. During my PhD, I have been fortunate to intern at NVIDIA Research and Meta AI (FAIR), and my research has been supported by the NVIDIA Graduate Research Fellowship. Before starting my PhD, I was a visiting student at UC Berkeley and a research intern at Berkeley AI Research. I received my BS and MS degrees from the Technical University of Denmark (DTU).
