I recently received my PhD from UC San Diego, where I was advised by Professors Xiaolong Wang and Hao Su. During my PhD, I have been fortunate to intern at NVIDIA Research and Meta AI (FAIR), and my research has been supported by the NVIDIA Graduate Research Fellowship. Before starting my PhD, I was a visiting student at UC Berkeley and a research intern at Berkeley AI Research. I received my BS and MS degrees from the Technical University of Denmark (DTU).

I am broadly interested in advancing machine intelligence, with particular emphasis on the following topics:

World models & RL
Newt, TD-MPC2, TD-MPC
Robot learning
FOWM, MoDem-V2, GNFactor
Generalization
Puppeteer, LfS, SODA, SVEA

Besides research I also like plants 🪴, books 📚, and they/them pronouns 🏳️‍🌈.
